# Roadmap

PatientMatch is early-stage open-source software. The near-term roadmap focuses on making the storefront easier to run, inspect, and adapt.

## Near Term

- Improve keyboard and screen-reader behavior in trial filters and screeners.
- Expand tests for `lib/pmqAdapter.ts` and match-profile deduplication.
- Add more synthetic questionnaire fixtures for edge cases.
- Improve empty states and patient-readable trial explanations.
- Keep dependency advisories resolved through compatible updates.

## Medium Term

- Add a fully scripted local Supabase demo setup.
- Improve condition pages with clearer patient education content.
- Add more robust map and proximity test coverage.
- Document deployment hardening for public-interest and advocacy deployments.
- Improve trial comparison workflows for caregivers and clinicians.

## Ongoing

- Keep service-role access out of storefront runtime code.
- Keep public issues and test data free of PHI/PII.
- Preserve the storefront/data-factory boundary.
- Review patient-facing language for accessibility, clarity, and medical-claim safety.
